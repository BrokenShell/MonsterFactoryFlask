Meta Magic

Summoning Ritual Invocation

  1. Pray to Fortuna, the great goddess of luck. You will need it.
  2. Inscribe the name of the monster you wish to summon at the altar.
  3. Choose the desired monster type from the book of all monsters index.
  4. Choose the appropriate power. Secondary stats will be set automatically.
  5. Modify secondary stats to taste and summon your new best friend.
  6. Run for your life! Newly summoned monsters are typically quite hungry.
  7. If you planned well, you only need to outrun your slowest companion.
